What Spargo does
Every feature built
to save you money

Price search

Search any item — groceries, gas, hardware — and see prices ranked cheapest first, filtered by distance from you.

Grocery list optimizer

Add your whole shopping list and Spargo tells you the single cheapest store — or the best split across multiple stores.

Exclusive offers

Get early access to flash sales and partner deals before they go public. Spargo-only discounts from local stores.

Price score

Scan or manually enter a price tag and get a score out of 100 — a full breakdown of whether you're actually getting a deal.

Proximity filtering

Sort deals and search results by distance. No point saving $0.50 when the store is 30 km away.

Barcode scanning

Point your camera at any shelf label or price sticker. Spargo reads it and instantly shows you better options nearby.
