Price search

Search any item — groceries, gas, hardware — and see prices ranked cheapest first, filtered by your distance.

List optimizer

Add your whole shopping list and Spargo finds the single cheapest store — or the best split across two stops.

Exclusive offers

Get early access to flash sales and partner deals before they go public. Spargo-only discounts from local stores.

Price score

Scan or enter a price tag and get a score out of 100 — a real breakdown of whether you're actually getting a deal.

Barcode scanning

Point your camera at any shelf label. Spargo reads it and instantly shows you better options nearby.
