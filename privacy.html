Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Spargo ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used when you join our waitlist at spargo.app.

What we collect

When you submit the waitlist form, we collect:

  • Your email address

We do not collect your name, location, payment information, or any other personal data through this website.

Why we collect it

Your email address is collected solely to notify you when Spargo launches and to send you early access information. By submitting the form you are providing express consent under Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) to receive these communications.

How we store and process it

Waitlist submissions are transmitted securely via EmailJS, a third-party email delivery service. Your email address is not sold, rented, or shared with any other third parties.

How long we keep it

We retain your email address until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Once Spargo launches, you may opt out of further communications at any time using the unsubscribe link included in every email.

Your rights

Under CASL and applicable Canadian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent and unsubscribe at any time
  • Request that we delete your email address from our records
  • Ask what personal information we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@spargo.app. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies, analytics, or local storage. No data is stored in your browser.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@spargo.app.
